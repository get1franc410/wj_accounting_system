About WJ-Accounting

Go Beyond Basic Bookkeeping.

Take full control of your business with a professional-grade accounting system.

WJ-Accounting is engineered for accuracy, compliance, and efficiency, giving you the tools to manage complex finances with ease and make data-driven decisions.

  • Full Double-Entry system with a customizable Chart of Accounts.
  • Track real-time inventory levels from purchase to sale.
  • (New) Manage production formulas, manufacture finished goods, and track costs.
  • Manage fixed assets and automatically calculate monthly depreciation.
  • Generate professional invoices, track payments, and manage receivables.
  • Produce key financial reports like Income Statements and Balance Sheets instantly.

Focus on growing your business while our system handles the complexities of financial recording and reporting. Make informed decisions backed by accurate, real-time data.

Core Features

Everything you need to manage your finances effectively.

Professional Accounting Core

Utilize a full double-entry system. Manage journal entries, customize your Chart of Accounts, and maintain a clean, auditable general ledger.

Integrated Inventory Control

Track product stock from purchase orders to sales invoices. The system automatically updates inventory levels and posts Cost of Goods Sold (COGS) entries.

Production & Manufacturing

Define production formulas, manufacture finished goods from raw materials, and automatically calculate the unit cost for accurate profit analysis.

Asset & Depreciation Management

Record fixed assets, track their value, and let the system automatically post monthly depreciation entries to the correct accounts for you.

Instant Financial Reporting

Generate essential reports like Trial Balance, Income Statement, and Balance Sheet with a single click, using real-time data from your transactions.
